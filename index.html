heima

A family oriented lodging app

Project type: Mobile app

Time: Summer 2020

Roles played: Research, analysis, development, prototyping, guerella testing

Summary

Travelling with children and finding a safe, suitable place to stay can be stressful. Today’s most popular travel and lodging apps provide a wide array of options but they don’t cater specifically to the needs of young families. heima (Icelandic for home) was developed to give busy parents the ability to discover, book, and review their homes away from home.

Challenge

Mobile apps have changed the way people travel and there are endless options available. After conducting extensive competitor analysis and numerous interviews with parents I learned these apps couldn’t address all their lodging needs. Are adjoining rooms available? Are local attractions within walking distance? Does the food service cater to specific dietary needs? What do other parents think of the accommodations?

Solution

I compiled my research data and developed a user persona to drive the ideation and development process. Fred Foster is a working professional with a wife and two children. The family loves to travel and Fred is looking for an app to make his life easier while doing so.
